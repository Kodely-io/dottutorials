## Introduction @unplugged

Welcome! We need to light up LED in front of home position and LED behind the home position.

## Step 1 @fullscreen

Drag and place the ``||dot:move forward||`` block in the ``||basic:on start||``.


```blocks
dot.moveForward()
```

## Step 2 @fullscreen

```blocks

```
